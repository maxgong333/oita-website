Our Story

Indulge in the exquisite
allure of Japanese cuisine

Our recipes have been passed down through generations — a family of renowned chefs ensuring a taste steeped in tradition, elevated for the modern table.

We started in Manhattan in 2014, spending 4 years perfecting our craft before bringing Oita to Park Slope, Brooklyn in 2018 — where we've been serving the community for over 6 years.
